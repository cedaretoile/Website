Cedar Etoile

Indigenous Photographer and Storyteller

Cedar Etoile is an Indigenous Photographer and Storyteller based on so-called Vancouver Island, BC. They take pride in being Kwakwa̱ka̱ʼwakw, Nuu-chah-nulth, Metis from the Dzawadenuxw, Ditidaht, Cree, and Metis Nations. Their background is in adventure & recreation tourism, brand strategy, advertisement, and marketing. Graduted from Vancouver Island Univeristy. Freelance content creator and Idnigenous consultant for Destination British Columbia, Tourism Vancouver Island, Visit Port Hardy, Tourism Nanaimo, Tourism Nanaimo, and Discover Campbell River.
